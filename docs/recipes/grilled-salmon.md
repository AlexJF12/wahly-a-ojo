# Simple Grilled Salmon

![Grilled salmon fillet](../images/grilled-salmon/hero.jpg)

## Ingredients

- 4 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each), skin on
- Olive oil
- Flaky salt and black pepper
- 1 lemon, cut into wedges
- Fresh dill or flat-leaf parsley (optional)

## Instructions

1. Pat salmon dry with paper towels. Brush both sides lightly with olive oil.
2. Season generously with salt and pepper — a ojo, use your judgment.
3. Heat grill to medium-high. Oil the grates well.
4. Lay salmon skin-side down. Grill 6–8 minutes without touching it.
5. Flip carefully and grill 2–4 more minutes. The fish is done when it just flakes.
6. Rest 2 minutes, then serve with lemon wedges and fresh herbs.

## Notes

Watch the sides of the fillet as it cooks — you'll see the flesh shift from translucent pink to opaque from the bottom up. Pull it off the grill when the opaque line reaches about three-quarters of the way up. Carryover heat does the rest.
