# Rice Krispy Treats

## Ingredients

- 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal
- 1 bag (10 oz) marshmallows
- 3 tbsp butter
- Pinch of salt
- 1/2 tsp vanilla (optional but better)

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a big pot over low heat. Add the marshmallows and stir constantly until they're completely melted and smooth. Take it off the heat.

2. Stir in the salt and vanilla if you're using it. Pour in the cereal and fold it together gently. You want every piece coated but you don't want to crush them.

3. Press into a buttered 9x13 pan. Don't press too hard — if you compact them, they'll be dense and chewy instead of light and crispy. Use a piece of wax paper or wet hands to pat them down.

4. Let them cool completely before cutting. Or don't. Nobody actually waits.
