# Chocolate Lava Cake

![Chocolate lava cake with molten center](../images/chocolate-lava-cake/hero.jpg)

## Ingredients

- 115 g dark chocolate (70% cacao), chopped
- 115 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 60 g sugar
- 30 g all-purpose flour
- Pinch of salt
- Cocoa powder for dusting
- Vanilla ice cream to serve

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 °F (220 °C). Butter four 6 oz ramekins and dust with cocoa powder, tapping out the excess.
2. Melt chocolate and butter together over a double boiler, stirring until smooth and glossy. Set aside to cool slightly.
3. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar together until the mixture thickens and turns pale, about 2 minutes.
4. Fold the chocolate mixture into the eggs gently. Add flour and salt, fold until just combined.
5. Divide batter evenly among the ramekins. (Can be refrigerated up to 24 hours at this point.)
6. Bake 10–12 minutes. Edges should be set but the center should still jiggle when nudged.
7. Let sit 1 minute, run a knife around the edge, and invert onto plates. Serve immediately.

## Notes

The doneness window is narrow — check at 10 minutes and every minute after. Every oven is different, so the first time you make these, sacrifice one cake a minute early to learn your oven. After that, you'll know by sight exactly when to pull them.
