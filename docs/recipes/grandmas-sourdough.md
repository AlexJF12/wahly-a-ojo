# Grandma's Sourdough Bread

![Finished loaf](../images/grandmas-sourdough/hero.jpg)

## Ingredients

- 500 g bread flour
- 350 g water
- 100 g active sourdough starter
- 10 g salt

## Instructions

1. Mix flour and water in a large bowl. Rest 30 minutes (autolyse).
2. Add starter and salt. Fold until fully combined.
3. Bulk ferment 4–6 hours at room temperature, folding every 30 minutes for the first 2 hours.
4. Shape into a boule and place in a floured banneton.
5. Cold retard in the fridge 8–16 hours.
6. Preheat oven to 500 °F with a Dutch oven inside for at least 45 minutes.
7. Bake covered 20 minutes, then uncovered at 450 °F for 20–25 minutes until deep golden.

![Crumb shot](../images/grandmas-sourdough/crumb-shot.jpg)

## Notes

The key to great sourdough is patience. Trust your instincts — a ojo — you'll know when the bulk ferment is done when the dough feels alive and bubbly under your hands. The crust should sound hollow when you knock the bottom.
