# Recipe Style Guide

Conventions for writing recipes in this collection. Read this before adding or editing a recipe.

## Recipe

- Maximum of 5 steps from start to finish.
- Casual tone of voice.
- Encourage using smell, sight, and touch.

## Unsolicited Opinions

- A conversation between 2 and 4 people. **The author cannot appear in the unsolicited opinions.**
- People in the conversation are drawn from: Nate, Alex, Ben, Tim, and Carolyn.
- Snarky and funny tone.
- Provide suggestions that genuinely make the dish better.
